Coach You Or Your Team In Rocket League











About:

I am Curly. I am an experienced player in Rocket League after playing for 5 years, with close to 4000 hours of game time. I've competed in ESL tournaments and a few smaller leagues. I reached Grand Champion in rocket league towards the end of season 12. In Season 13 i managed to reach top 100 on Xbox in both 2s and 3s. Towards the end of season 13 i moved to PC where i hope to improve my ability as a rocket league player and coach. 



I specialize in giving practical advice to improve quickly in Rocket League.



I am able to cater to all skill levels.



Curriculum depends entirely on the skill level of the customer. The goal is to create maximum value for the customer and therefore every coaching session is customized to your specific needs.



Example topics:

Optimizing settings.

Drills to improve mechanical skill. Improving mechanical skills is key to becoming a good player in Rocket League. The right drills help a lot in achieving it. Practical advice for strategy (team and individual) e.g. rotations etc.



Feel free to ask me anything.
